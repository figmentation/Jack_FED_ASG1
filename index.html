About us

Welcome to Magiska, where we bring magic and wonder to your special moments! Founded by Finn Frost, a former farmer born in the icy expanse of Antarctica, Magiska is a unique magician-for-hire service with a story as extraordinary as the performances we deliver. Born amidst the endless ice and snow, Finn’s childhood was far from ordinary. While most children played in fields, Finn played with illusions in his mind, mastering sleight of hand with snowflakes and creating enchantments out of the frozen landscape. As he grew, he traded his farming tools for a magician’s wand and started perfecting his craft, inspired by the beauty and mystery of the Antarctic world. After years of studying ancient illusions and performing for small gatherings, Finn founded Magiska to share the joy and wonder of magic with the world.
